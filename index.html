Privacy Policy for JobHunter Extension

Last Updated: September 2025

The JobHunter Extension does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

Local Processing Only

All CV data, preferences, and job scoring are processed entirely within your browser. Nothing is sent to external servers.

No Tracking

The extension does not track your browsing activity.

No Data Storage Outside Your Device

Any saved preferences (such as keywords or score thresholds) are stored locally in your browser storage and never leave your device.

Third-Party Services

The extension interacts only with Indeed job listings to display results.
